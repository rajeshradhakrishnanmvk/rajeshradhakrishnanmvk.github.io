### FAQ

**Is this actually free?**
Yes. The monthly story is completely free. No credit card required. You'll also get occasional offers for new releases and bundles, but you can ignore them — the free story arrives regardless.

**What if I already bought your books on Amazon?**
Thank you! The Vault gives you stories that aren't on Amazon — subscriber-exclusive content you can't get anywhere else.

**How often will you email?**
Once a week. New release alerts, story recommendations, behind-the-scenes content, and the monthly free story. No daily spam.

**Can I unsubscribe?**
One click. No hard feelings. The darkness will miss you.

**Why are you doing this?**
Because Amazon takes 30-65% of every sale. When you're in The Vault, you hear about new releases directly — and if you buy from this store, the author keeps ~95%. Same price. Same book. Radically better for the writer.
