# Product Template — Individual Book
# Customize in: Online Store → Themes → Customize → Products → Default product

---

## Product Page Layout

### Product Media (Left)
- Book cover image (3:4 ratio, high-res — at least 1600x2400px)
- Optional: Alternate cover, back cover, interior spread
- Product image zoom: enabled

### Product Info (Right)
- **Vendor:** Dark Corners Books
- **Title:** [Book Title]
- **Price:** $2.99 (ebook) / $12.99 (paperback)
- **Format selector:** Variant picker — "Ebook" / "Paperback" / "Signed Paperback"
- **Quantity:** Hidden (books are single-purchase)
- **Buy button:** "ADD TO CART" — styled as .product-form__submit in CSS
